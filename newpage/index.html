!!! Important !!! Read the FAQ, it'll help you fix many common issues you might get.

A particularly common issue is not seeing your site changes immediately after reload, this is possibly because of browser caching, where you dont get your new files loaded.
A simple fix is to just press Ctrl + Shift + R or Ctrl + f5 depending on you browser. This will clear the cache and load your latest changes.
